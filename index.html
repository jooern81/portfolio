My Work

Who I am

Tutor, personal trainer, engineer, data analyst...

I worked at the Ministry of Transport Futures Division for half a year, formulating drone regulations and introducing applications to the government sector. This was followed by 2 years in the military where I was commissioned as an officer (Lieutenant) and served in the Combat Service Support HQ as a manpower officer. After this I took up the Singapore Industries Full Term Scholarship and majored in Electrical Engineering, with a minor in Product Design at the Singapore University of Technology and Design. During my studies, I had the privilege of completing an exchange program at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Upon graduating Summa Cum Laude, I served my 4-year bond at Singapore Mass Rapid Transit in maintenance and condition monitoring roles as a Senior Executive Engineer and completed a postgraduate diploma in Urban Railway Engineering with the University of Birmingham.

I am currently completing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Singapore Management University with a specialization in Data Science and Engineering. In my free time, I program, run, drum and work on random projects for fun.
